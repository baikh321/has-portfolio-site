I’m an Experience Designer from Hong Kong living in Boston, where I work as a Senior Experience Designer at DigitasLBi.

Designing interfaces and experiences that solve real problems for users is what drives me everyday, and I constantly nerd out about making them simple and easy to use. As a maker with a background in industrial design, I bring the lens of ergonomics, intuitive design, and cradle-to-cradle systems into the digital world. Whether I am designing a website or furniture, I believe that good design results in a good user experience.
