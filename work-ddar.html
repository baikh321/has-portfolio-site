Dunkin’ AR Innovation

Blending together the digital and physical worlds of Dunkin’

Smarter Packaging

Using emerging technologies such as Augmented Reality, we created three experiences to make you savor going to Dunkin’ even more. It made sense to use packaging as an extension of the brand, and foster genuine engagement with customers through that connected layer.

Our initiative of designing interactive packaging allows users to connect in deeper and richer ways to the brand.

The Process

I used several different augmented reality programs, including Aurasma, Augment, and Blippar. Blippar was the only tool that enabled full customization with code, allowing me to build all 3 AR experiences using JavaScript.

Concept 1: Tic-Tac-Toe

Bored and waiting in line? Scan your DD Card and unlock a game of Tic-Tac-Toe. Have what it takes to beat the AI? Win and get rewarded a free beverage!

Concept 2: Patriot's No.1 Fan

Show your support and celebrate Dunkin’ Donuts “Patriots Win, You Win” campaign with New England Patriot’s Rob Gronkowski. Snap and share your victory pose!

Concept 3: Pumpkin Builder

Show Dunkin’ your creative side by celebrating Halloween with a box of donuts and a mess-free jack-o-latern carving.
